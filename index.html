Contáctame

Luciano Moore

Diseñador y Programador Web

I was born in Marcos Juarez, Cordoba, in 1995. At the age of 18 I moved to the capital to study Industrial Design at the Faculty of Architecture, Urbanism and Design of the UNC. As I advanced in my career I went deeper and deeper into digital design and the different areas it covers. Currently I am part of the development team of a large Argentine company in the agribusiness sector, where I apply my knowledge of 3D and interface design.

Faced with the panorama of great technological advances and the impact it has on my profession, is that I have been training as a Systems Analyst at the prestigious Faculty of Astronomy, Mathematics and Physics of the UNC. This and a series of programming courses with different languages (JavaScript, Python, Ruby, C) mark another possible path that I am eager to follow.
